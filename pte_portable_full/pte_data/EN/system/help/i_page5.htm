Atomic Structure
Click anywhere in the 'black box' area to toggle the animation.
The atomic structure diagram displays a visual representation of the electron structure of an element. The electrons are shown orbiting the nucleus. The number of neutrons and the number of protons in the nucleus is shown.
Please be aware that this is not to be taken literally, it has been known for a long time that electrons do not orbit the nucleus in this way.
